Personal Experience

I am Viktoria Titova, the Creator, Owner, and CEO of this brand. My role involves weaving traditional craftsmanship with contemporary design, managing sustainable supply chains, and sharing our story worldwide. Guided by effectual entrepreneurship, I adapt swiftly to market changes and shape opportunities from available resources.

“I’ve never worn clothing that felt so alive with history,” says Maria K., an early customer from Berlin. “Each thread connects me to a global community of makers and dreamers.”
